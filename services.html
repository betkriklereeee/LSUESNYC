Locksmith Services on the Upper East Side

From emergency lockouts to high-security installations — we cover every locksmith need for residents and businesses across ZIP 10128.

Why Local Matters

A locksmith who knows your building, your block, and your lock

Most of the Upper East Side runs on the same handful of lock systems — Mul-T-Lock cylinders in prewar co-ops, Medeco deadbolts on brownstone entry doors, Schlage hardware in newer condos. We've been servicing these buildings since the early 1990s. When you call us, you're not getting a dispatch center routing a stranger to your door. You're getting a technician who has likely worked in your building before.

We're also straightforward about what a job requires. If your lock needs repair rather than replacement, we'll tell you. If a key can be duplicated in-store rather than ordered, we'll do it on the spot. No unnecessary upsells, no inflated emergency rates — just honest service at a fair price.
