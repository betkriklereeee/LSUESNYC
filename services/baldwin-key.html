Baldwin Key Cutting & Lock Service in NYC

Baldwin hardware is built to last generations. We cut Baldwin keys with the precision the brand demands — and service the full range of Baldwin locks, handlesets, and deadbolts.

Call Now: (917) 338-1950

Precision service for a precision brand

Baldwin has built its reputation on craftsmanship — solid brass hardware, tight tolerances, and finishes that hold up over decades. A key cut on a poorly calibrated machine won't meet that standard. It'll work, until it doesn't — wearing the cylinder prematurely or failing at the worst possible moment.

We cut Baldwin keys on calibrated equipment using the correct blanks for each profile. The result is a key that operates smoothly from day one and maintains that feel over time. We also carry a selection of Baldwin handlesets, deadbolts, and decorative hardware in-store, and our locksmiths are familiar with the full Baldwin product line.

Baldwin services we offer

Key duplication

Exact copies cut to Baldwin's tolerances — guaranteed smooth operation from the first use. Standard and decorative profiles available.

Lock installation

New Baldwin deadbolts, handlesets, and entry sets installed correctly — proper alignment, correct backset, full function from day one.

Rekeying

Moving into a new home or want to retire old keys? We rekey Baldwin cylinders to a new key profile without replacing the hardware.

Lock repair & maintenance

Stiff operation, misalignment, worn internal components — we diagnose and repair Baldwin locks rather than defaulting to replacement.

Baldwin on the Upper East Side

Baldwin hardware is common in the Upper East Side's brownstones, co-ops, and landmarked buildings — particularly on entry doors and interior doors where aesthetics matter as much as security. We've serviced Baldwin hardware throughout the neighborhood and know the profiles and configurations most common to NYC residential buildings.

If you're not sure whether your lock is Baldwin or what profile your key is on, bring it in — we'll identify it on the spot and tell you exactly what your options are.

Why the right blank matters

Baldwin keys use specific keyway profiles that require the correct blank to cut properly. Using a generic substitute blank introduces small dimensional differences that cause uneven wear on the cylinder pins — leading to a key that feels slightly off and eventually fails to turn reliably.

We stock genuine Baldwin-compatible blanks and cut to spec. It's a small detail that makes a meaningful difference over the life of the lock.
