Fast, non-destructive lockout service across New York City

Getting locked out is stressful — and the last thing you need is a technician who damages your door to get in. Our locksmiths are trained in non-destructive entry techniques, meaning we pick or bypass your lock without drilling or forcing the mechanism. In most cases, your lock is left fully functional after we're done.

We're based on the Upper East Side and serve all five boroughs. Whether you're locked out of a prewar co-op, a brownstone, a newer condo, or a commercial property, we know the lock systems common to NYC buildings and carry the tools to handle them on the first visit.

What kinds of lockouts do we handle?

We handle residential and commercial lockouts of all types — including apartment doors, building entry doors, mailboxes, interior office doors, and glass entry systems. If you've broken a key off in the lock, we can extract it. If your lock has seized or malfunctioned, we can repair or replace it on the spot.

We work with all major lock brands common to NYC buildings — Mul-T-Lock, Medeco, Schlage, Baldwin, Sargent, Corbin Russwin, and more.
