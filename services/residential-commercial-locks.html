Residential & Commercial Lock Installation in NYC

From a single deadbolt upgrade to outfitting an entire building — we supply, install, and service locks for homes, apartments, offices, and commercial properties across all five boroughs.

Call Now: (917) 338-1950

Supply, installation, and service — under one roof

Most locksmiths will install a lock. Fewer carry meaningful inventory of their own and can advise you on which lock is right for your door, your building, and your security requirements. We do both — we stock hardware from the brands we know and trust, and our technicians install and service what we sell.

That means no middlemen, no waiting on orders, and no disconnect between the person who sold you the lock and the person who installs it. If something isn't right, we make it right.

Residential vs. commercial — what's the difference?

Residential

  • Apartment and co-op entry doors
  • Brownstone and townhouse hardware
  • Deadbolts, handlesets, and knobsets
  • High-security upgrades for NYC apartments
  • Mailbox and interior door locks
  • Historic and decorative hardware

Commercial

  • Office and storefront entry locks
  • Panic bars and exit devices
  • Cylindrical and mortise commercial locks
  • Glass door and frameless entry systems
  • Warehouse and industrial hardware
  • Multi-unit building common area locks

Hardware types we carry & install

Deadbolts

Single and double cylinder deadbolts for residential and light commercial doors — the most common upgrade request on the UES.

Mortise locks

Standard in NYC prewar buildings — we install, repair, and replace mortise lock bodies and cylinders for both historic and modern applications.

Cylindrical locks

Grade 1 and Grade 2 cylindrical locksets for commercial doors — offices, suites, and interior access points.

Handlesets & entry sets

Baldwin, Schlage, and Medeco entry sets — installed with correct backset and strike alignment for smooth operation.

Panic bars & exit devices

Von Duprin, Sargent, and Corbin Russwin exit hardware — code-compliant installation for commercial properties.

High-security cylinders

Mul-T-Lock, Medeco, and 248 Lock cylinders — retrofit into existing hardware or as part of a full lock replacement.
