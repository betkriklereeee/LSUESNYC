Key Duplication in NYC — Precision Cuts for Every System

From standard house keys to high-security Mul-T-Lock and Medeco systems — we cut keys to exact manufacturer specification, guaranteed to work on the first try.

Expert key cutting for any lock system

Most key duplication shops can handle standard house keys. We go further — our machines are calibrated for the full range of residential, commercial, and high-security key systems common to NYC buildings. Whether you need a single spare or a full set for a building with 30 units, we cut every key to the tolerances the lock demands.

We're an authorized Mul-T-Lock dealer operating the official KC5™ machine, which means high-security Mul-T-Lock keys cut here are done under proper key control protocols — not workarounds. Same for Medeco, Baldwin, Schlage, and Ilco blanks, which we stock in-store and cut while you wait.

High-security key control — why it matters

High-security keys like Mul-T-Lock and Medeco use patented keyways and restricted blanks. This means they can only be duplicated by authorized dealers — protecting you from unauthorized copies being made without your knowledge. When we duplicate a high-security key, we verify authorization and maintain a record under the system's key control protocol.

If you're not sure whether your key is restricted, bring it in — we'll tell you exactly what system you're on and what your duplication options are.
